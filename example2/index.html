Common Questions

Everything You
Need to Know

Have more questions? Our friendly team is happy to chat before you book — no obligation, just honest answers.

Call Us
Do you accept health fund cover?
+
Yes — we accept all major Australian health funds and offer HICAPS on-site so you only pay the gap on the day. We'll always provide a cost estimate before treatment so you know exactly what to expect.
Are you accepting new patients?
+
Absolutely. We welcome new patients of all ages and do not require a referral. Simply book online or give us a call and we'll get you in as soon as possible, often same week.
What if I'm nervous about dental treatment?
+
We specialise in anxious patients. We offer happy gas (nitrous oxide), headphones, and take a completely patient-led pace. Just let us know when you book and we'll make sure your appointment is designed around your comfort.
Do you offer payment plans?
+
Yes — we offer interest-free payment plans through ZipPay and Afterpay for treatments over $200. For larger treatment plans, we can also arrange extended payment schedules directly through our practice.
How often should I have a check-up?
+
For most adults, a check-up and clean every 6 months is ideal. Patients with higher risk of decay or gum disease may benefit from more frequent visits. We'll recommend a recall interval based on your individual oral health needs.
